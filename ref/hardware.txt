Disable screen from blanking on raw terminals:
  setterm -powerdown off -blank 0

Disable X11 from blanking the screen:
  In /etc/X11/xorg.conf, under "Monitor", include:
    Option "DPMS"
  And under ServerLayout, include:
    Option "BlankTime" "0"
    Option "StandbyTime" "0"
    Option "SuspendTime" "0"
    Option "OffTime" "0"
